## ğŸ› ï¸ Usage

1.  **Install & Open Relay**.
2.  **Add a Server**:
    *   Click "Add Server" to configure one manually.
    *   Or check the "Marketplace" to find pre-configured servers.
3.  **Enable Servers**: Toggle the switch to enable a server.
4.  **Export**:
    *   Go to **Settings** and click "Export to Claude" to sync your config.
    *   Relay can also auto-sync changes immediately.
